# About this guide

**Persona:** Advanced coder who is comfortable with meta-programming and template languages.

**Scope:** How to install, render templates, and integrate the core library or CLI. Design rationale and maintainer workflows live in [features](../features/index.md) and [developer](../developer/index.md).

**Syntax focus:** [Legacy syntax](../glossary/legacy-syntax.md) is stable and used by the CLI. [Modern syntax](../glossary/modern-syntax.md) is experimental.
